# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the spirit of 996 culture, we pledge to:

- 🕘 Work hard, play hard (but mostly work hard)
- 🔫 Be honest and direct when someone's code sucks
- 📊 Focus on measurable improvements and outcomes
- ♾️ Strive for continuous improvement through healthy involution

## Our Standards

### ✅ Good Behavior
- Constructive criticism that makes the code better
- Healthy competition that drives everyone forward
- Clear and direct technical feedback
- Sharing knowledge and best practices

### ❌ Unacceptable Behavior
- Writing obviously bad code and expecting praise
- Overengineering to show off
- Not adopting competitor's good ideas just out of pride
- Personal attacks or harassment of any kind

## Our Responsibilities

The CTO is responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards.
In extreme cases, employees may be eliminated (from the contributor list).
