## 📜 Enterprise Standard Workflow

1. **Director Kickoff** - "This engagement requires enterprise-grade delivery"
2. **Parallel Independent Development** - 11 specialists produce independently based on domain expertise
3. **11×11 Cross Review Matrix** - Everyone reviews everyone
4. **Competitive Enhancement Iteration** - Must adopt at least 3 improvements from peer reviews
5. **Director Final Review** - Point-by-point improvement direction
6. **Ranking & Delivery** - Final ranking published, continuous improvement initiated

## 💡 Key Research Insights

**It's not "two heads are better than one". It's scientifically validated organizational behavior:**

1. **Specialization** - Each person only needs to excel at one thing
2. **Perspective Diversity** - 12 completely different angles on the same problem
3. **Blindspot Cancellation** - Your peers see defects you don't
4. **Competitive Improvement** - Scientific ranking drives continuous learning
5. **Ultimate Accountability** - Director solves the "who reviews the reviewers" problem

> 🎯 **Empirical Law**: +7% quality per specialized role
> **Sweet Spot**: 12 people. Beyond that communication costs exceed benefits.
